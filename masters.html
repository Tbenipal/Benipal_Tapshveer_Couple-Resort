Summary of Master

Experience opulent Old European style at our boutique lakeside retreat, perfect for romantic getaways. Nestled in Algonquin Park, Muskoka, indulge in the freshest air and serene surroundings from your private outdoor hot tub. Our Romantic Master's Jr Suite boasts luxurious features including a whirlpool bath tub, steam room, and four shower heads. Adorned with furnishings from The Art Shoppe of Toronto, escape into tranquility and elegance. Unwind amidst the enchanting ambiance and luxurious accommodations, discovering a haven of sophistication unlike any other.
