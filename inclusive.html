Summary of All-Inclusive

Ultimate romance and luxury. Indulge in a sumptuous 5-course gourmet dinner served in our elegant dining room or as convenient takeout room service. Immerse yourself in relaxation with our private outdoor hot tub, positioned on the edge of nature overlooking the serene lake. Cozy up beside a romantic real wood-burning fireplace, effortlessly easy to use and authentically charming. To ensure your experience is nothing short of perfect, feel free to bring your own favorite wine for dinner and special bubbles for the in-room whirlpool bath, while we provide the firestarter and complimentary firewood. As a luxury couples-only retreat, we pride ourselves on being the ideal destination for your special getaways. Nestled amidst the breathtaking beauty of Algonquin Park, our resort offers a plethora of opportunities for fresh air, wildlife sightings, scenic trails, serene lakes, captivating museums, and inspiring artist galleries. Come escape to paradise with us, where every moment is tailored to create unforgettable memories of romance and relaxation.

Summary of All-Inclusive

At our resort, indulge in a myriad of recreational activities, all with complimentary gear provided. From the thrill of pickleball, tennis, and badminton on regulation-size asphalt courts to serene adventures gliding across the water in kayaks, canoes, or paddle boards, there's something for everyone. Stay cozy with our free clothing rentals, including everything from hiking boots to windproof and waterproof pants and jackets. Explore the landscape on our Norco 21-speed mountain bikes or engage in a relaxing fishing excursion with our equipment available for $20 per day. For those seeking a bit of adventure, horseback riding experiences await. Delight in our range of motor boat options, catering to all preferences and budgets. Please note, however, that there are certain limitations and exclusions. Additionally, for winter enthusiasts, we offer complimentary ice hiking gear and winter clothing rentals, ensuring a comfortable and enjoyable experience. Embrace the magic of Algonquin Park with these exciting activities and gear options, and don't miss the chance to feed the famous wildlife birds right from your hands during this daily activity.
